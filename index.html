Biography

  • I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Engineering from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of California, Irvine, starting from Fall 2012. I am working with my advisor, Professor Brian Demsky, in the Programming Language Research Group.

  • I received my B.S. in Software Engineering in 2012 from the School of Software Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in Shanghai, China. I worked with Professor Jianjun Zhao in the STAP group.

Research Interests

  • I mainly focus on building reliable concurrent software and systems, including static/dynamic analysis and tool support. More specifically, I am actively involved in the verification and testing of concurrent data structures under relaxed memory models.
